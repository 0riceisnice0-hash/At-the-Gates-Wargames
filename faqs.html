Questions About Selling Your Collection

Pricing depends on what you're selling, its condition, and current market demand. Painted armies are assessed on painting quality and cohesion. Unpainted models are valued based on condition and completeness.

We provide realistic offers based on what we can realistically sell the collection for, not inflated retail prices. Contact us with photos and a description for a specific quote.

Both. Quality paint jobs add value—we'll pay more for well-painted, cohesive armies than for tabletop standard or rushed work. Unpainted models are valued on completeness and condition. Built but unpainted is fine. New-on-sprue is ideal.

We're honest about what painting adds value and what doesn't.

We buy models in any reasonable condition. Broken models have less value. Missing parts affect pricing. Stripped or heavily converted models are harder to sell, so we pay less.
