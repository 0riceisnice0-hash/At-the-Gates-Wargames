Our Services

Straightforward buying and selling for Warhammer and tabletop miniature collectors.

We Buy Warhammer Collections

We purchase Warhammer 40K, Age of Sigmar, and older Games Workshop miniatures across Hampshire and the South East. Whether you have a single painted army or an entire collection spanning multiple systems, we'll provide a fair valuation based on current market conditions and actual collector demand.

What We Look For

Painted Armies

Quality paint jobs add value. We assess painting standard, army cohesion, and basing when making offers on painted collections.

Unpainted Models

New-on-sprue, built but unpainted, or partially painted models all considered. Condition matters more than completion.

Bulk Collections

Large multi-army collections welcome. We'll travel for substantial collections worth the journey across the South East, South West, or London areas.

Specialist Games

Necromunda, Blood Bowl, Kill Team, Warcry, and older Games Workshop systems. We know the niche market and price accordingly.

Large Collection Valuations

Got a substantial collection and want to know what it's worth? We provide realistic valuations for large Warhammer and tabletop miniature collections. This isn't eBay pricing or retail value—it's what you can actually expect to receive from a fair buyer in the current market.

Valuation Process

1. Initial Contact

Send us photos and a brief description of your collection. We'll give you an initial assessment within 24 hours.

2. Detailed Review

For large collections, we'll arrange to view in person. We travel across Hampshire, South East, South West, and London for collections that warrant it.

3. Fair Offer

We'll make a cash offer based on actual market value. No obligation to accept, and we'll explain our pricing if you want to understand the valuation.

We Travel for Collections

Based in Hampshire, we regularly travel across the South East and into the South West and London for collections worth the journey. Typically this means collections valued at £500 or more, but we're flexible based on what you're selling.

Coverage Areas

Primary Area: Hampshire

Southampton, Portsmouth, Winchester, Basingstoke, and surrounding areas. We'll travel locally for smaller collections.

Extended Coverage

Berkshire, Surrey, West Sussex, Dorset, Wiltshire, and Greater London. Larger collections only—contact us to discuss.
